## 💡 Pro Tips

### Technical Excellence:
1. **Always explain your reasoning** - Don't just give answers
2. **Discuss trade-offs** - Show you understand complexity
3. **Think out loud** - Let interviewer follow your thought process
4. **Ask clarifying questions** - Shows thoroughness
5. **Mention real-world experience** - Connect theory to practice

### System Design:
1. **Start with requirements** - Clarify before designing
2. **Draw diagrams** - Visual communication is key
3. **Estimate capacity** - Show you think about scale
4. **Identify bottlenecks** - Proactively address issues
5. **Discuss monitoring** - Production-ready mindset

### Behavioral:
1. **Use STAR method** - Structure your stories
2. **Quantify impact** - Use metrics and numbers
3. **Show leadership** - Even as individual contributor
4. **Be honest** - Don't fabricate stories
5. **Prepare 10+ stories** - Cover different scenarios
