## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency > Intensity**
   - Study 4-5 hours daily
   - Don't skip days
   - Take short breaks

2. **Active Learning**
   - Code along with examples
   - Explain concepts out loud
   - Teach someone else

3. **Mock Interviews**
   - Practice with peers
   - Record yourself
   - Get feedback

4. **Portfolio Quality**
   - Production-ready code
   - Good documentation
   - Deployed and accessible

5. **Health & Rest**
   - 7-8 hours sleep
   - Regular exercise
   - Healthy meals
