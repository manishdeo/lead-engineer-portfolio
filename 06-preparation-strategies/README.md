## 💡 Quick Tips for Lead Roles

- **Focus on Trade-offs:** Always present multiple solutions and articulate *why* you chose a specific one.
- **Show Ownership:** Emphasize projects where you drove the architecture from conception to production.
- **Think at Scale:** Be ready to scale any system to 1M+ DAU automatically.
- **Mentor and Lead:** Have stories ready about how you mentored juniors or resolved team conflicts.
